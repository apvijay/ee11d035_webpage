Applying for financial grant

To be submitted to Academic Section (4th floor). Ms.Regina is the in-charge for EE Ph.D. But you need to drop your envelope with all the letters and enclosures into the tapal box near the 4th floor entrance.

Required letters:

  • Cover letter to Dean AR
  • DC recommendation letter
  • Institute financial grant template
  • Bona fide request letter to DR (Acad)

All forwarded through the guide and the HOD.

Enclosures:

  • Conference acceptance letter (email and/or webpage print)
  • Abstract of your paper
  • Grade sheet
  • Comprehensive completion letter
  • Accommodation details (copy of hotel webpage with price, e.g. booking dot com)
  • Visa fee (copy of webpage)
  • Air India quotation

Plan:

  • Go to Air India office and submit the quotation request. You may be asked to come after two or three days to collect.
  • Send mails to DC members and get appointments for signatures on DC reco letter.
  • Go to Acad section (4th floor), and submit the gradesheet request. If lucky, you will be given the gradesheet immediately.
  • If compre-pass letter is not with you, go to the department office, and ask them to give you a copy of it. It will be present in your file.
  • Check vfsglobal website for visa fees.
  • Check booking dot com website for acco price.
  • Request an amount greater than ₹1.5 lakhs so that they would give ₹1.5 lakhs, else you might get lesser.

Visa application (for example to Zurich, Switzerland)

Required letters:

  1. Cover letter to Visa officer, Swiss Consulate

Enclosures:

Check the vfsglobal website for the required documents for the visa. Commonly required documents are given below.

  • Passport in original, with two less-than-six-months-old photographs
  • Copy of first and last pages of the passport
  • Invitation letter from conference chairs
  • Conference registration email proof/receipt
  • Copy of institute identity card
  • Original bonafide and no-objection certificates from DR(Acad)
  • Institute financial grant letter and bank amount transfer email
  • Bank statement six-month history
  • Air ticket (Chennai to Zurich, and Zurich to Chennai)
  • Hotel accommodation booking in Zurich (booking dot com)
  • Travel insurance (check amount in visa checklist of vfsglobal)

Plan:

  1. Get bonafide and NOC (single certificate) from acad section (You'd have received as a part of applying for financial grant).
  2. Register for the conference, and get the receipt/email.
  3. Request an invitation letter for visa from conference chairs. Send them your details (name, passport number, hotel you are going to stay at Zurich, address, institute) if asked. They would send you by email, and they also may send a copy to the consulate directly.
  4. Book the hotels; try booking dot com, hostels dot com
  5. Book flight tickets; check air india website itself, or for any local flights at Zurich, check swiss dot com, bravofly, eithad airlines, darwin airlines etc.
  6. Go to All India Travel office near Guru, give them your details (name, father's name, address, passport number) and apply for a travel insurance (usually silver plan) for the full number of days at Zurich. Put some extra days if needed. Usually it will be in terms of 7. Check also the amount needed to be covered in the visa checklist. It is 30k euros for Schengen visa.
  7. Before one or two days of going to the visa application centre, go to SBI with the print of your historical statement online. Get it signed from the service manager (extreme right after entering). Check your name and roll number are correct in your bank details (name and address fields), if not, change them beforehand. Give a requisition letter to the person sitting to the extreme left of the bank, in a small open area with a table. They will take a day at most. If you are lucky, they may change the details in front of you. You have to submit a copy of your id card.
